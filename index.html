Welcome to Blackjack

Blackjack is a popular card game where the goal is to beat the dealer's hand without going over 21.

Card Values

Cards 2-10 are face value, face cards are worth 10, and Aces are worth 1 or 11.

Gameplay

You can 'Hit' to take another card or 'Stand' to hold your total. Try to get as close to 21 as possible.
